About Me



I’m a first year PhD student at Brown CLPS advised by Bertram Malle, studying social norms in the context of Human Robot Interaction. I also work at the Human Centered Robotics Initiative.

In Spring 2019, I graduated from University of Michigan with double majors in Computer Science and Cognitive Science, minors in Linguistics and German studies. As an undergrad, I worked at Professor John Laird’s SOAR group at UM.

Apart from research, I enjoy watching artistic movies, travelling and equestrian.
